Ingredients:
 - 1/2 pkg ground beef
 - 1 teaspoon parsley flakes
 - 1 teaspoon oregano
 - 1/4 pkg of whole-grain pasta
 - 6 cups of water
 - 1/2 jar of Ragu pasta sauce

Directions:
 1. Brown the ground beef in a large pot and drain off the excess fat.
 2. Add the pasta sauce to a sauce pan over low heat for about 20 minutes.
 3. Add the parsley, oregano, and water to a large pot and bring to a boil.
 4. Add the pasta to the pot of water and boil on medium-high for about 12 minutes.
